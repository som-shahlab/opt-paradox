Treatment:  The recommended treatment for appendicitis is appendectomy. This can be performed laparoscopically or via an open incision.  Laparoscopic appendectomy is generally preferred due to its less invasive nature, resulting in faster recovery, less postoperative pain, and reduced risk of wound infections.
